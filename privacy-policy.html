​1. Introduction and Our Commitment to Your Privacy
​Welcome to Inbondz News. Our mission is to provide news that is open and accessible to all. Because we are an open-source news platform, we do not require any user accounts, logins, or registration to access our content.
​This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your privacy while you use our website.
​2. Information We Do Not Collect
​Inbondz News is committed to minimal data collection. Specifically:
​We do not collect or store personal identifying information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number because we do not have a registration or login process.
​We do not store passwords or credit card information as we do not process financial transactions or maintain user accounts.
​3. Information We Do Collect (Usage Data)
​When you access the Site, we automatically collect certain non-identifying information about your device and usage for the purpose of improving our service, troubleshooting, and understanding reader trends. This Usage Data may include:
​IP Address: Used to determine general geographic location (country/city) for analytics, but not linked to your identity.
​Browser Type and Version: Information about the software you are using to view the site.
​Pages Viewed: The specific articles or pages you visit on the Site.
​Time and Date of Visit: When you accessed the Site.
​Referring/Exit Pages: The website that referred you to Inbondz News and the page you exited to.
​Device Information: Type of device (mobile, desktop, tablet) and operating system.
​4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
​What are Cookies? Cookies are small files placed on your device. They are used to help a website function efficiently and improve user experience. ​Our Use: We use cookies primarily for functional and analytical purposes.
​Functional Cookies: To ensure the website loads and displays correctly.
​Analytical Cookies (e.g., Google Analytics): To understand overall site traffic, article popularity, and visitor trends (e.g., how many people read a story). This data is aggregated and anonymized where possible.
​Your Control: You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.
